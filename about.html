who we are

A Trusted Service Provider since 2018 - Malar Hospital

We began our journey in 2018 as a small multispecialty clinic with a simple vision, to offer accessible, quality healthcare to our community. Driven by patient trust and clinical excellence, we’ve since grown and transformed into an 80-bedded multispecialty hospital, bringing all major specialties under one roof. Today, with over 50,000+ patients served, we stand as a testament to compassionate care, medical expertise, and a commitment to continuous growth — without ever losing sight of our patient-first values.

Founder's Message

When I founded Malar brand back in 2018, my goal was clear — to create a healthcare institution that not only treats illness but also educates and empowers patients to take charge of their health and well-being.

As the need for advanced and integrated care grew, I was inspired to expand this vision and hence, this journey evolved into the creation of Malar Hospital. A place where advanced facilities & multi-specialty care come together under one roof, ensuring every patient receives seamless, coordinated, and comprehensive care.

Built on the pillars of quality, teamwork, depth of care, and trust, Malar Hospital is more than just a place for treatment. It’s a centre of healing where every patient is heard, every life is valued, and every effort is made to deliver the best possible outcome.

We remain committed to guiding patients not just through recovery, but through awareness, prevention, and lifelong well-being.

Warm regards,

Dr. B. Kathiravan

Our Values

At Malar, we live and breathe our values of Collaboration, Empowerment, Dignity, and Respect while delivering the highest quality of care. We have an open-minded approach, embrace cultural diversity, and provide services that are comprehensive and culturally appropriate.

Our Vision

Being a trusted healthcare partner with a focus on innovative solutions to improve the health and well-being of the community through advanced technology and compassionate care delivery.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional patient centric care through quality infrastructure, compassionate service, and healthcare education through empathetic communication with patient.

About CAHO

The Consortium of Accredited Healthcare Organizations (CAHO) is a not-for-profit society committed to improving healthcare quality and patient safety in India. It collaborates with hospitals, labs, and healthcare professionals to promote standards through accreditation, training, and continuous improvement.

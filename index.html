Frequently Asked Questions ?

Get quick answers about our services, facilities, and patient care at Malar Hospitals.

(01)

What types of diagnostic services are available at Malar Hospitals?

Malar Hospitals offers a wide range of diagnostic services, including CT Scan, Digital X-ray, Ultrasound, ECG/ECHO, Laboratory testing, Audiometry, and Endoscopy to ensure accurate and timely healthcare decisions.

(02)

Is emergency care available at all times?

Yes, Malar Hospitals provides 24/7 Emergency Services and Pharmacy access, along with reliable Ambulance support, ensuring prompt and expert care around the clock.

(03)

Do you offer specialized care for newborns and critical patients?

Absolutely. Malar Hospitals has dedicated Intensive Care Units (ICU), including a specialized Neonatal ICU, equipped with advanced technology and expert medical staff to provide critical and compassionate care.
